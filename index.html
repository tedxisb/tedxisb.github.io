The host school

Indian School Of Business

ISB evolved from the need for a world-class business school in Asia. The founders, some of the best minds from the corporate and academic worlds, anticipated the leadership needs of the emerging Asian economies. They recognised that the rapidly changing business landscape would require young leaders who not only have an understanding of the developing economies but who also present a global perspective. ISB is committed to creating such leaders through its innovative programmes, outstanding faculty and thought leadership. Funded entirely by private corporations, foundations and individuals from around the world who believe in its vision, the ISB is a not-for-profit organisation.

What's TEDx?

Ideas worth spreading

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxIndianSchoolOfBusiness, where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDxIndianSchoolOfBusiness event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.

Esteemed Speakers

Speaking @TEDxIndianSchoolOfBusiness are

Director of the social enterprise ‘Navnirmati Learning Foundation’, Ms Mahashabde started her journey in education & science 20 years ago. Greatly influenced by the National Science Movement, her organization brings sustainable changes in how education is delivered to the children of our country. Championing the cause of “Quality” in education, her organization has impacted several schools & hundreds of children by altering pedagogy & teaching techniques.

Ms. Geeta Mahashabde

It is hard to imagine the grit & determination that went in transforming the sixth daughter of an agricultural farmer from Imphal into an Arjuna Award winning boxer. But Ms. Devi’s story is just that. There is more to this national champion, and former Lightweight World Champion, than meets the eye. A mother, a fighter, a champion – she is all this & more. Hear her speak about her journey, her struggle and the unbelievable success she has achieved at TEDxIndianSchoolofBusiness!

Ms. Sarita Devi

Nipun Malhotra, born with Arthrogryposis (a rare congenital disorder leading to a lack of muscles in arms and legs) is an inspiring story of how the differently abled can overcome all odds to lead a fulfilling and dignified life. This St. Stephen's and ISB MFAB Alumnus is the founder and Managing Trustee of the Nipman Foundation through which Nipun has been working tirelessly to provide opportunity and dignified living for the differently abled through raising awareness, providing a link between corporates and the differently abled.

Mr. Nipun Malhotra

One of the leading performers of the Kuchipudi art form, Guru Deepika Reddy truly symbolizes worship for art. The countless awards and recognitions conferred upon her include the “Kala Ratna”, the highest award in Andhra Pradesh and ‘Nritya Choodamani’ by Krishna Gana Sabha With a passion to take Kuchipudi to the next generation, Guru Deepika founded ‘Deepanjali’, institute striving for excellence in the field of Kuchipudi which is currently training 165 students and created performances that won the hearts of both audience and critics.

Guru Deepika Reddy

The City of Nawabs is embellished with most intricate monuments and sculptures. Over 137 monuments are under the Hyderabad circle of Archaeological Survey of India. It is the continuous dedication of few men that keeps these monuments as scintillating as ever. Babji Rao is an Archaeologist and a personification of that dedication. With invaluable insights from his career spanning decades, he is set to bring alive glimpses of our awe-inspiring heritage in his talk.

Babji Rao

Pioneer of the rock-based Punjabi ballads, Rabbi Shergill is a well-known name in the world of music. Inventing the perfect blend of poetic Punjabi with rock music, Rabbi Shergill has continued to create soulful music with socially relevant lyrics -- "Bulla ki jaana" et al. The son of a Sikh preacher and a Punjabi poet, Rabbi Shergill brings together Rock, Sufi and Punjabi folk to create magic. We might just find out what is the secret ingredient behind that magic, in his talk.

Rabbi Shergill

A stand-up comedian, environmentalist, rock climber, triathlete. Phew! If you think that's too much of activity for one person, she is also a ballerina and has danced with Bolshoi. Oh wait, she teaches, too, at the Xavier Institute of Management - Bhubaneswar, and she's also the founder of the non-profit Thinmakka’s Resources for Environment Education in the US. At TEDxIndianSchoolOfBusiness, she would talk about topic currently being debated all over - freedom of expression.

Vasu Primlani

Sameer Nair is the CEO of Balaji Telefims. Formerly, the CEO of Star Entertainment, Sameer Nair played an important role in the revolutionizing the viewership of the channel with the legendary shows of “Kaun Banega Crorepati” and “Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi”. Having created a huge success at Star, Sameer Nair now joins Balaji Telefilms to scale up their movie business. Sameer has also been a part of the Aam Admi Party to create the same impact in Indian Politics.

Mr. Sameer Nair

Dr. Vikas Amte is the administrator of Anandwan, or Forest of Joy, a small world created for leprosy patients, completely self-contained providing livelihood to over 5000 people. Dr. Vikas Amte is the elder son of Baba Amte and has continued his father’s noble work through humanitarian activities endorsing “Parity not Charity”. A visionary and medical expert, Dr. Amte has been awarded the prestigious Lokmanya Tilak award along with his younger brother. Come listen to him narrate the story of Anandwan and its serene life which brings peace and joy beyond our imagination.

Dr. Vikas Amte

Heard of the prestigious Jagriti Yatra, the revolutionary Anna Hazare movement and the recent historical success of the AAP government? What these three galvanizing events have in common – Ruben Mascarenhas. A Computer Engineer by qualification, Project Manager by profession and a pioneer by heart, the versatile Ruben is currently the Media Secretary for AAP Maharashtra. A leading example of the growing prominence of Youth in Indian Politics, who better than Ruben to provide invigorating insights about the “The obscure face of Indian Politics” on the TEDx stage!

Ruben Mascarenhas

Man of the Year by the American Biographical Institute, Ex-Director, Central Forensic Science Lab, CBI and Ex-offcio Chemical Examiner to the Govt. of India, Mr. Sehgal wears many hats with comfort. It probably comes from years of practice, and an unrelenting passion for unearthing the truth. Delve into the mysterious world of forensics, criminal investigation & more with a seasoned practitioner. A man who has traveled the globe, worked with the likes of Scotland Yard and seen more than an 18 minute talk can fit in!

Mr. V.N.Seghal
